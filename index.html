Token Male

Would you like a chance the finish your sentence? We've got you covered.

Our fully-trained, white skinned men stand ready to lend you their aura of authority and credibility. They will accompany you to your next meeting, investor pitch, or average day at the office. They come in various outfits and have been groomed to perfection.



Tired of people talking over you? Ignoring your input? Or mistaking you for an assistant? We understand. Ideally, whether you are taken seriously or not does not depend on your race, or gender. In fact, we hope to one day arrive in a world where this service would be completely redundant.

Until such time, this is our solution for an imperfect world: white, generic looking men to accompany you to your meetings and presentations. Their one and only job is to redirect focus to you, talk you up and interrupt your interrupters.

Book a man

We offer two types of men: the grey-haired, suit-and-tie wearing, seasoned business-man and the young, hoodie-wearing coder type.

However, don't assume either of these types holds any of the corresponding skills. Their only job is to redirect attention to you. If someone cuts you off mid-sentence, they will merely say something along the lines of, 'she was still speaking' so that you can go back to pitching your idea, or giving your opinion.

All our men are volunteers. Please treat them with the respect they deserve.

Become a volunteer

Are you a white man and eager to put your privilege to good use? We're looking for you!

To become a volunteer you'll receive a day long training, during which you'll be made aware of your (unconscious) biases and learn how to function in a supporting role. After that you'll be ready to take as many appointments as you like, to help non-white males everywhere get their messages out!
